THE PHILIPPINES POVERTY

POVERTY

IN THE PHILIPPINES, 18.1% OF THE POPULATION LIVED BELOW THE NATIONAL POVERTY LIVE IN 2021.

Poverty in the Philippines is one of the most serious problems that the government must deal with. In relation to its nature, poverty in the Philippines arose from the rapid population growth in the country. Poverty, defined by lack of resources for necessities like food, clothing, and shelter, extends beyond financial insufficiency to hunger, as per the World Bank Organization. The majority of Filipinos are regarded as being poor. And it is really difficult to believe that the innocent kids are the ones who suffer the most. They begin learning how to make a living at a very young age, both for themselves and their families. They are already vulnerable to illnesses or mishaps that could kill them and their future selves at a very young age. Without play, childhood, or education, there would be no advancement.
